## FAQ

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| How does Lupe actually work? | Lupe connects to your existing data infrastructure (BigQuery, Snowflake, or your own warehouse) and reads data from your ad networks, MMPs, and revenue platforms. We build a unified semantic layer on top of your data, then apply purpose-built AI models for daily insights, predictive forecasting, driver analysis, and natural language analytics. Your data never leaves your infrastructure. `[CHANGED: added "purpose-built" and "driver analysis"]` |
| What makes Lupe different from a BI tool or dashboard? | BI tools show you what happened. Lupe tells you *why* it happened, what's likely to happen next, and what to do about it. We decompose metric changes into root causes, forecast where performance is heading, and deliver daily briefings with prioritized actions — not just charts. `[NEW]` |
| I already have a data team — does it still make sense to work with Lupe? | Yes. Lupe doesn't replace your data team — it gives them leverage. Instead of spending cycles on recurring reports and ad-hoc queries from marketing, your data team can focus on deeper projects while Lupe handles the day-to-day intelligence layer. |
| I have only part of my funnel data in-house — can I still use Lupe? | Absolutely. Lupe works with whatever data you have and helps you build toward a more complete picture over time. We typically start with ad spend and attribution data and expand from there. |
| What's the advantage compared to building in-house? | Building a custom analytics layer requires dedicated engineering time, ongoing maintenance, and months of iteration. Lupe gives you a production-ready intelligence layer with predictive models, driver analysis, AI chat, and daily briefings from week one. You get the output of a senior analytics team without the 6-month build. `[CHANGED: "MCP server or data pipeline" → "in-house", added "driver analysis"]` |
| Will Lupe fit our tech stack and tools? | Lupe is designed for the mobile marketing ecosystem. We integrate with major ad networks (Facebook, TikTok, Google, AppLovin, Unity), MMPs (Adjust, AppsFlyer), analytics platforms (Amplitude, Firebase, Snowplow), revenue tools (RevenueCat), and data infrastructure (BigQuery, Snowflake, AWS, dbt). `[CHANGED: added Snowflake]` |
| Does Lupe work with Slack? | Yes. Daily digests, alerts, and answers to ad-hoc questions can be delivered directly in Slack. Your team gets the same analysis quality as the dashboard, where they already work. `[NEW]` |
| Why would I need a data model for attribution? | Privacy changes (ATT, SKAdNetwork, Privacy Sandbox) have made platform-reported metrics unreliable. A proper data model lets you build your own attribution logic using first-party data, so you're not relying solely on what each ad network tells you about its own performance. |
| How quickly will I see results? | Customers typically identify their largest sources of budget waste and highest-performing channels within 90 days of connecting their data. Many see initial insights — like flagged underperforming campaigns and unified cross-channel views — within the first week. |
