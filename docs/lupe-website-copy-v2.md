| Step | Title | Description |

| 01 | Connect your data | Lupe plugs into your existing stack: ad networks, MMPs, analytics tools, revenue platforms. Your data stays in your warehouse. We read it, we don't move it. |

## Built for Scale *(NEW SECTION)*

- **Heading:** Built different.
- **Subheading:** Not a dashboard. Not a wrapper. An analytics infrastructure layer that adapts to your data.

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Schema-driven** | Lupe reads your data model and adapts automatically. Add new metrics, dimensions, or data sources — the platform picks them up without code changes. No hardcoded logic. |
| **Your warehouse, your data** | Lupe connects to BigQuery, Snowflake, or your existing warehouse. Your data never leaves your infrastructure. We query it in place. |
| **Multi-model AI** | Different analytical tasks need different AI approaches. Lupe routes planning, analysis, forecasting, and conversation to purpose-built models — not one general-purpose LLM doing everything. |

## FAQ

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| How does Lupe actually work? | Lupe connects to your existing data infrastructure (BigQuery, Snowflake, or your own warehouse) and reads data from your ad networks, MMPs, and revenue platforms. Your data never leaves your infrastructure. |
| What makes Lupe different from a BI tool or dashboard? | BI tools show you what happened. Lupe tells you *why* it happened, what's likely to happen next, and what to do about it. Daily briefings and alerts mean your team acts on insights, not just looks at charts. |
| I already have a data team — does it still make sense to work with Lupe? | Yes. Lupe doesn't replace your data team — it gives them leverage. | What's the advantage compared to building in-house? | Building a custom analytics layer requires dedicated engineering time, ongoing maintenance, and months of iteration. Lupe gives you a production-ready intelligence layer with predictive models, driver analysis, AI chat, and daily briefings from week one. You get the output of a senior analytics team without the 6-month build. |
| Will Lupe fit our tech stack and tools? | Lupe is designed for the mobile marketing ecosystem.
